# EmailFlow: Optimal Email Management System

EmailFlow is an optimal email management suite designed to streamline your email experience. It automatically organizes, prioritizes, and optimizes your emails, helping you stay focused and efficient.

## Features

- **Managing Email Overload:** Automatically organizes and prioritizes emails based on relevance and urgency.
- **Improved Productivity:** Categorizes and prioritizes emails for efficient handling.
- **Effective Time Management:** Integrates email content into your calendar for seamless scheduling.
- **Spam and Phishing Detection:** Filters out malicious emails using advanced ML algorithms.
- **Space Optimization:** Reduces storage requirements through data preprocessing and compression.
- **Text Summarization:** Provides concise email summaries for quick understanding.
- **Personalization:** Adapts to your preferences for personalized notifications and prioritization.
- **Enhanced Decision-Making:** Prioritizes emails to ensure timely actions.
